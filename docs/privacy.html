Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Learn with Velmorth ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App"), our spaced repetition systems, and our learning features.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data. By downloading and using the App, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect certain information that you provide directly to us or that is generated automatically when you utilize our language learning features:

  • Account Information: When you sign up or log in, we collect your name, email address, and authentication tokens via safe authentication frameworks.
  • Learning Progress and Statistics: We track your lesson history, XP (experience points), learning streaks, Spaced Repetition System (SRS) intervals, categories studied, and general learning progress to tailor your education.
  • Device & Usage Information: We automatically collect device characteristics, OS version, regional settings, and generic usage logs to debug performance and prevent application crashes.

How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected information to deliver a comprehensive, seamless, and personalized language learning platform:

  • To authenticate and manage your user account securely.
  • To compute SRS retention intervals, tracking precisely when you need to review vocabulary.
  • To reward your progress, calculate daily streaks, and display leaderboards/progress charts.
  • To verify subscription status and enable premium content via secure app-store integration.
  • To detect, prevent, and troubleshoot software errors and server latency.

We do NOT sell, lease, or distribute your personal contact information to any third-party marketing companies.

Third-Party Services

To ensure high-performance operation, crash-free loading, secure payments, and standard ad monetization, the App integrates reliable third-party software development kits (SDKs). These third parties may collect and process information as governed by their respective privacy policies:

Firebase Auth & Analytics
Used for secure email authentication, user session persistence, and aggregate, non-identifying usage statistics to refine features.
Firebase Crashlytics
Automatically logs call stacks and device performance metrics during unexpected exceptions to help us deploy quick fixes.
Google AdMob
Monetizes free features by displaying relevant, non-intrusive educational advertisements. They may use anonymized advertiser IDs.
Google Play Billing
Securely handles subscriptions and premium paywall transactions. We never see or store your credit card details.

Cookies and Ad Tracking

Mobile applications do not utilize standard web browser cookies. However, Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics may make use of mobile-specific identifiers, such as the Google Advertising ID (AAID) for Android, to serve personalized ads or trace performance trends.

You can easily opt-out of personalized tracking or reset your advertising identifier directly inside your device's native settings (under Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Data Retention and Deletion

We store your account credentials and language study statistics for as long as your account remains active. If you wish to delete your account and wipe all associated study data (progress, XP, streak history) from our servers, you can initiate a deletion request at any time.

To request permanent account deletion, please email us directly from your registered email address at the contact address listed below, and we will purge your records within 7 business days.

Children's Privacy

Learn with Velmorth is an educational tool suited for learners of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has registered without verified parental consent, we will delete their account details immediately.

Your Rights

Depending on your geographic location (e.g., European Union GDPR or California CCPA), you may hold specific legal rights concerning your personal information. These generally include the right to access a copy of your data, request modifications to inaccurate profiles, or mandate the complete deletion of your records.

Changes and Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to accommodate adjustments to native software features or legal updates. When we perform updates, we will adjust the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
